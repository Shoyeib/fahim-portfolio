Probable Applications

IoT Gateways

Use Wi-Fi/Bluetooth from ESP32-WROOM and CP2104 USB-UART for seamless wireless-to-USB communication in smart homes or edge devices

Smart Lighting or Actuator Control

ULN2003A LED driver supports control of high-current outputs such as LEDs, buzzers, or solenoids

Environmental Monitoring

Integrated SI7021 sensor allows accurate temperature and humidity measurement for weather stations or smart agriculture

USB-Based Communication

CP2104 chip offers reliable USB-to-serial bridging for PC communication, debugging, and firmware updates

Embedded System Prototyping

With its modular components and protection, this board is ideal for rapid prototyping in both lab and field environments

Portable Electronics

TLV1117LV LDO and USC 2.0 connector enable stable low-voltage operation and compact power delivery for mobile/embedded devices
