Efficient power transmission over long distances

Project Overview

High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) technology enables efficient transmission of large amounts of electricity over long distances with lower losses compared to traditional AC systems. This project demonstrates the implementation and testing of an HVDC system prototype.

Our tests confirmed that the HVDC boost converter efficiently steps up low DC voltage to high levels with reliable and stable performance. The circuit maintained consistent output under varying load conditions, proving its suitability for small-scale high-voltage applications.
